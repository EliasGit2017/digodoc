.  matches any character
 *  matches the preceding expression 0, one or multiple times
 +  matches the preceding expression one or multiple times
 ?  Matches the preceding expression once or not at all
 ^  matches at the beginning of line
 $  matches at end of line
 \|  alternative between two expressions
 \  quotes special characters previously described
 [a-z], [0-9]  ranges \[a-z\] (or \[0-9\]) to match a character from a set
